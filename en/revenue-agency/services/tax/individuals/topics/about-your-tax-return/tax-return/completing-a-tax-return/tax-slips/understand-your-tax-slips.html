Tax slips: Understand your slips

From Canada Revenue Agency

  • Get a copy of your tax slips
  • Understand your tax slips

Slips are normally prepared by your employer, payer, or administrator.

You should get most of your slips and receipts by the end of February. However, T3, and T5013 slips come later, in early April.

Tax slips

  • T4 slips (T4, T4A, T4A(OAS), T4A(P), T4E, T4RIF, and T4RSP)
  • T5 slips (T5, T5007, T5008, and T5013)
  • T3 Statement of Trust Income Allocations and Designations
  • RC62 Universal Child Care Benefit statement
  • RC210 Working Income Tax Benefit Advance Payments Statement
  • RRSP contribution receipt – slip information for individuals
